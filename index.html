Matthew Chan

Pre-Business

Matthew Chan graduated from Glen A. Wilson high school in Hacienda Heights in 2021. During his time there he was involved with the Associated Student Body where he held the position of athletic commissioner his sophomore year. In addition to this, he was selected by his fellow students to be their student body Vice President during his junior year. In his junior year he strengthened his skills as a leader by hosting rallies, meeting with leaders from neighboring schools, and leading meetings. This prepared him for his elected role as student body President in his senior year where he attended a two day training and collaborative event with other Presidents from various parts of California.

Matthew now attends the University of California Riverside where he majors in Pre-business. He has recently joined the Associated Students Program Board, an organization tasked with bringing entertainment to the campus, as a volunteer in the events team. Here he works large events such as concerts.

Matthew has volunteered for various organizations throughout his years, such as YWCA and Food Forward. During his time with the YWCA he helped the organization work towards their cause, which was to give girls in low income areas as well as girls in third countries the opportunity to find a passion in STEM. To do this, he generated login information for participants as well as filming demonstration videos on how to use certain equipment. While working with Food Forward, Matthew would attend weekly farmers markets to gather and distribute unwanted items to local food banks to give back to their communities.
